I've recently fallen in love with a racing simulator called [iRacing](https://www.iracing.com/). I already had a wheel and pedal tucked away in a closet, so I pulled them out and set up iRacing with my Oculus Rift. What a blast! I love the competitive nature and the ability to work to slowly raise my skill level.

I quickly found that I didn't like having to attach and detach the racing wheel to my desk, so I picked up the [Playseat Challenge](https://www.playseatstore.com/playseat-challenge.html), which is a folding chair designed just for racing. It makes it very simple to setup for racing and tear down when I'm done.
